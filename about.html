About Us

Have you wanted to settle the score with your partner, friend, or foe? Well we sure have. That’s how we came up with The Score Settler. What started as a simple discord bot now turned into a multi-trillion dollar corporation with 20,000 stores worldwide. We the creators, Noël Khalaf and Vincent Vu, are humbled by our simple beginnings and strive to bring about fairness into the world one coin flip at a time.
